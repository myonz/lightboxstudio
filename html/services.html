Our Services

Professional Product Photography & Videography

What We Offer

Comprehensive visual content solutions for your brand

Product Photography

High-quality product shots with professional lighting and post-production. Perfect for e-commerce, catalogs, and social media marketing.

Product Videography

Dynamic product videos that showcase your items in motion. Ideal for social media campaigns, advertisements, and website content.

Food Photography

Mouth-watering food photography that makes your dishes irresistible. Specialized lighting techniques to capture textures and colors perfectly.

Fashion Photography

Professional fashion and apparel photography with model or mannequin styling. Showcase your clothing line with stunning visuals.

Creative Direction

Complete creative consultation and art direction for your brand. We develop concepts that align with your marketing goals.

Post Production

Advanced photo and video editing, color grading, and retouching. We ensure every frame meets professional commercial standards.

Cara Kerja

Proses terstruktur dari briefing hingga final delivery

  1. 01
    Briefing

    Diskusi kebutuhan, konsep, dan tujuan visual untuk memahami brand dan produk Anda secara mendalam.

  2. 02
    Konsep & Planning

    Pembuatan mood board, storyboard, dan perencanaan teknis untuk memastikan hasil sesuai ekspektasi.

  3. 03
    Production

    Sesi pemotretan atau videografi dengan tim profesional dan peralatan studio kelas atas.

  4. 04
    Post-Production

    Editing, retouching, color grading, dan finishing untuk menghasilkan visual berkualitas tinggi.

  5. 05
    Review & Revision

    Presentasi hasil dan revisi sesuai feedback untuk memastikan kepuasan maksimal.

  6. 06
    Final Delivery

    Pengiriman file final dalam berbagai format sesuai kebutuhan untuk berbagai platform.
